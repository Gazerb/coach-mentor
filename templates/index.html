Clitheroe Wolves FC Coach Mentor System

Welcome to the Clitheroe Wolves coach mentor booking system. As a member coaching team we are here to help with your coach development. One of the things we can offer is for a coach mentor to provide you with support during your time with us. Once registered you can then make a booking with the team and a member will be in touch to confirm the booking and prepare.

We also offer advice on any issues you may be struggling with i.e. player issues or parental issues. Just email on of our metors from the metors page and they will be in touch to provide some advice or point you in the relevant direction.
